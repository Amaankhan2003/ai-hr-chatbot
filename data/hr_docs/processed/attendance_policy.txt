Policy Name: Attendance Policy

Purpose:
This policy defines the rules and expectations related to employee attendance and punctuality.

Working Hours:
The standard working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are expected to log in on time and complete their assigned working hours.

Attendance Marking:
Employees must mark their attendance using the official attendance system provided by the company.
Attendance should be marked at the start and end of the workday.

Late Arrival:
Employees arriving more than 15 minutes late will be marked as late.
Frequent late arrivals may result in disciplinary action.

Absenteeism:
An employee will be considered absent if attendance is not marked for the day and no prior approval is taken.
Unapproved absence may lead to loss of pay.

Regularization:
Employees can regularize missed or incorrect attendance entries through the attendance portal.
Regularization requests must be submitted within 3 working days.

Compliance:
Failure to comply with the attendance policy may lead to corrective action by HR.
