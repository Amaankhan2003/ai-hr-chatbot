Policy Name: Work From Home Policy

Purpose:
This policy defines the guidelines for employees working remotely.

Eligibility:
Employees may opt for work from home based on project requirements and manager approval.
Not all roles are eligible for work from home.

WFH Duration:
Employees are allowed up to 8 work from home days per month.
Additional work from home days require special approval.

Working Hours:
Employees working from home must adhere to standard working hours.
Availability during core business hours is mandatory.

Performance Expectations:
Employees are expected to maintain the same level of productivity while working remotely.
Regular updates should be provided to the reporting manager.

Security:
Employees must ensure company data is accessed only through secure devices and networks.
Sharing information while working remotely is strictly prohibited.
