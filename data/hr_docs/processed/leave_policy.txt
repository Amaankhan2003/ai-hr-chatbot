Policy Name: Leave Policy

Purpose:
This policy outlines the types of leave available to employees and the rules for availing them.

Casual Leave:
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per calendar year.
Casual leave is intended for short-term personal needs.
Unused casual leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.

Sick Leave:
Employees are entitled to 10 sick leaves per year.
Sick leave can be used in case of illness or medical emergencies.
Medical certificates may be required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.

Earned Leave:
Employees earn 15 earned leaves per year.
Earned leave can be accumulated and carried forward up to a maximum of 45 days.

Leave Application Process:
All leave requests must be submitted through the official leave management system.
Leave should be approved by the reporting manager before availing.

Unapproved Leave:
Leaves taken without approval may be treated as loss of pay.
